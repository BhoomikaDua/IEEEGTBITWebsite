IEEE Orientation 2018

10:30 AMAuditorium, GTBIT

IEEE Orientation is going to held for the freshers batch so as to make them aware of what is IEEE as a society as well as an organization. The students will also be taught about IEEE and its benefits. This orientation will include an overview of IEEE membership and access instructions for your members-only benefits and services.

September

08

PERFORM(G)IT Workshop

01:30 AMIEEE Room, GTBIT

IEEE GTBIT brings workshop PERFORM(G)IT to enlighten you all on GIT, How to contribute in Open Source and Pbench(Performance Tool of RedHat).It is an incredible opportunity for coding enthusiasts to learn about contemporary tools useful to them in their professional journey. The session will be commenced by one of our illustrious alumni who is currently working at Redhat. The workshop will be free of cost and open for IEEE members only.

October

03

IEEE Day 2018

10:30 AMLincoln High School

The IEEE Day team is made up of student volunteers, young professionals, and staff. Together, they work to assure that this year's celebration will be even bigger and more impressive than the last editions. The principle task of the IEEE Day team is to initiate, motivate, and coordinate events and efforts to celebrate this day worldwide.In addition to a enjoyable time and empowering members to engineer the future and beyond, the team actively supports members and volunteers.

IEEExtreme 2018

10:30 AMLincoln High School

IEEEXtreme is a global challenge in which teams of IEEE Student members – advised and proctored by an IEEE member, and often supported by an IEEE Student Branch – compete in a 24-hour time span against each other to solve a set of programming problems.
